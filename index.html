Who We Are

25+ Years of Excellence in Industrial Automation

L TORK Controls is a trusted manufacturer of electrical actuators, limit switches, and industrial control solutions. Since 2000, we have been delivering reliable, high-performance products that support engineering, automation, and process industries across diverse sectors.

With over 25 years of expertise, we combine advanced manufacturing, precision engineering, and stringent quality standards to develop products that ensure safety, durability, and dependable performance in demanding industrial environments. Our commitment to innovation and customer satisfaction has earned us the trust of clients across India.

  • 25+ years of manufacturing excellence
  • Wide range of electrical actuators and limit switches
  • Precision-engineered and quality-tested products
  • Reliable solutions for industrial automation and process control
  • Custom product development to meet application-specific needs
  • Dedicated technical support and prompt nationwide delivery
